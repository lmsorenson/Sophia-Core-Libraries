\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A concrete implementation of the \doxylink{classsophia_1_1monte__carlo_1_1models_1_1RolloutStrategyInterface}{Rollout\+Strategy\+Interface} that selects an action randomly. 

This strategy provides a simple and fast way to run simulations by choosing actions uniformly at random from the list of available options. It is often used as a default or baseline strategy in MCTS.
